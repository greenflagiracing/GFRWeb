To be added to the official list of paid drivers, please post a screenshot of your payment receipt in our private `#payment-confirmation` channel on Discord. An admin will then verify your entry.

ABOUT GREEN FLAG RACING

Founded in 2023, Green Flag Racing began as a community for friends racing in the F1 games. After expanding to Assetto Corsa, we've set our sights on the pinnacle of sim racing with our move to iRacing. Our mission is to provide a fair, competitive, and professionally managed racing environment for drivers of all skill levels who share a passion for clean, hard-fought battles on track.
